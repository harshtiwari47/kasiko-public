Kasiko Bot Command Guide

All commands are triggered by "kas .command". Use the available commands and aliases below.

User Commands
Command Alias Usage & Description
profile p View your profile or mention a user to view theirs. Usage: kas .profile [@user]
leaderboard lb View the leaderboard. Usage: kas .leaderboard
daily dr Claim your daily login reward. Usage: kas .daily
cash c Check your current cash balance. Usage: kas .cash
charity cy Check your charity balance. Usage: kas .charity
trust ts Check your trust level. Usage: kas .trust
roses r View your roses or Give roses to another user. Usage: kas .roses <amount> <@user>
Game Commands
Command Alias Usage & Description
tosscoin tc Play a coin toss game with a wager. Usage: kas .tosscoin <amount>
guess g Guess a number with a wager. Usage: kas .guess <amount> <number>
Shop Commands
Command Alias Usage & Description
shop shp Open the shop. Specify car or structure to view categories. Usage: kas .shop car
cars car, cr Access car-related commands. Usage: kas .cars
structures st, structure Access structure-related commands. Usage: kas .structures
buy by Buy an item from the shop. Usage: kas .buy <item> <id>
sell sl Sell an item back to the shop. Usage: kas .sell <item> <id>
Stock Commands
Command Alias Usage & Description
stock s View available stocks. Usage: kas .stock
stockprice sp Check the price of a stock. Usage: kas .stockprice <ticker>
buystock bs Buy a specified amount of stock. Usage: kas .buystock <ticker> <amount>
sellstock ss Sell a specified amount of stock. Usage: kas .sellstock <ticker> <amount>
portfolio pf View your or another user's stock portfolio. Usage: kas .portfolio [@user]
Ocean Life Commands
Command Alias Usage & Description
zone z List available zones to explore. Usage: kas .zone
explore ex Explore a specific zone. Usage: kas .explore <zone>
catch ch Catch an animal in the ocean. Usage: kas .catch
collect ct Collect rewards from your aquarium. Usage: kas .collect
collection cn View your or another user's animal collection. Usage: kas .collection [@user]
aquarium aq View your aquarium. Usage: kas .aquarium
aqua a Manage aquarium animals. Add, remove, or sell animals. Usage: kas .aqua <add|remove|sell> <animal> [amount]
feed f Feed animals in your aquarium. Usage: kas .feed <animal> <amount>
Marriage Commands
Command Alias Usage & Description
marry my Propose marriage to another user. Usage: kas .marry <@user>
divorce div Divorce your spouse. Usage: kas .divorce <@user>
marriage m, spouse View your marriage status. Usage: kas .marriage
